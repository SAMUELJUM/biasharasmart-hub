🚀 Quick Start

Get set up in 5 steps

Follow these steps after this tour to get the most out of BiasharaSmart Hub from day one.

1
Add your business profile — go to Business in the sidebar. Fill in your name, sector, location, and M-Pesa details.
2
Add your products to Inventory — so you can track stock levels and use the Point of Sale (POS) panel on your dashboard.
3
Record your first sale — click "Add Sale" in the sidebar and enter any recent transaction to get started.
4
Add your expenses — rent, stock purchases, utilities. This is how you calculate your real profit accurately.
5
Check Analytics — once you have some data, Analytics will show trends, AI forecasts, and business insights.
🎉

You are all set!
