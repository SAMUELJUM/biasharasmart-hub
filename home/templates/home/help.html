Getting Started — Registration

Create your account in under 3 minutes

Before you can use BiasharaSmart Hub, you need to create a free account. Registration only requires your phone number.

How to Register

#ActionWhat to do
1Open the websiteGo to the BiasharaSmart Hub website and click "Get Started" or "Register".
2Choose your planSelect Free, Pro, or Enterprise on the pricing page. You can upgrade later.
3Fill in your detailsEnter your First Name, Last Name, Username, Phone Number (format: 254XXXXXXXXX), and optional email.
4Set a passwordCreate a password of at least 8 characters. Re-enter it to confirm.
5SubmitClick "Create Account". You will be taken to the OTP verification page.
💡
Pro Tip: If you arrived from the pricing page by clicking a plan, your plan is pre-selected and shown in a banner at the top of the form.
⚠️
Important: Your phone number (254XXXXXXXXX) is your login username. It cannot be changed after registration.
02

Verifying Your Phone (OTP)

Confirm your identity with a one-time code

After registration, a 6-digit OTP is sent to your phone via SMS. You must verify before accessing the dashboard.

OTP Verification Steps

#ActionWhat to do
1Check SMSLook for a 6-digit code from BiasharaSmart Hub on your phone.
2Enter the codeType the 6-digit OTP on the verification page. It expires in 5 minutes.
3SubmitClick "Verify". If successful, you are logged in and taken to your dashboard.
4Resend if neededDid not receive the SMS? Click "Resend OTP". Check your number was entered correctly.
⚠️
Security: Never share your OTP with anyone. BiasharaSmart Hub staff will never ask for your OTP.

Setting Up Your Business Profile

Tell the system about your business

06

Recording Sales

Track every shilling you earn

Every sale should be recorded. Use the Add Sale form for detailed records, or the POS panel on your dashboard for quick counter sales.

Method 1: Add Sale Form

#ActionWhat to do
1Click "Add Sale"Click "Add Sale" in the left sidebar.
2Select businessChoose which business this sale belongs to.
3Enter amountType the total sale amount in KES.
4Select payment modeChoose Cash, M-Pesa, Bank Transfer, Credit, or Other.
5Add descriptionWrite what was sold, e.g. "3 bags of maize flour".
6SaveClick "Record Sale". Dashboard stats update immediately.

Method 2: Point of Sale (POS)

#ActionWhat to do
1Open POSThe POS panel is on your dashboard. Search for a product or click "Add to Cart".
2Adjust quantitiesUse + and - buttons to set how many units the customer is buying.
3Choose paymentSelect Cash or M-Pesa.
4Complete SaleClick "Complete Sale". Stock is automatically deducted from inventory.
💡
No internet? Record sales via USSD by dialling *123# from any phone.
07

Recording Expenses

Track what you spend to know your true profit

Record every business cost: rent, stock purchases, salaries, utilities, transport, etc. Without expenses, your profit figure will be wrong.

How to Record an Expense

#ActionWhat to do
1Click "Add Expense"Click "Add Expense" in the left sidebar.
2Enter amountType the expense amount in KES.
3Select categoryChoose: Rent, Stock Purchase, Utilities, Salaries, Transport, Marketing, or Other.
4Add descriptionDescribe clearly, e.g. "Monthly shop rent — January 2026".
5SaveClick "Record Expense". Profit calculations update immediately.
08

Managing Inventory (Stock)

Always know what you have in stock

Adding a New Product

#ActionWhat to do
1Go to InventoryClick "Inventory" in the left sidebar.
2Click Add ProductClick the "+ Add Product" button.
3Enter detailsName, selling price, buying price, current quantity, and unit (pieces, kg, etc.).
4Set reorder levelThe minimum quantity that triggers a low-stock alert (e.g. set 5 to alert when stock drops below 5).
5SaveClick "Save Product". It now appears in inventory and the POS panel.
💡
POS Integration: Products in Inventory automatically appear in the POS panel. Completing a POS sale auto-deducts stock.
